Frequently Asked Questions

This app helps users browse, buy, rent, and manage properties. Property owners can list their properties, and potential buyers or tenants can contact them directly through the app.

To log in, click on the "Login" button at the top right of the website and enter your credentials. To log out, click on your profile menu and select "Logout."

On the property details page, you can find the "Contact Owner" button. Clicking it will allow you to send a message or call the owner directly if a phone number is provided.

After logging in as a property owner, navigate to the "Add Property" section. Fill in the required details, upload images, and submit your listing. Once approved, it will be visible to other users.

Ensure that you are using the correct email and password. If you’ve forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it.
